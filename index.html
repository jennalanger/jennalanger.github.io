About

Waypoint Logic represents my love for technology, the outdoors, and charting my own way. My career path is filled with switchbacks, awe-inspiring views, flowy and technical sections, not to mention a few false summits. Each intersection represented a change in direction, helping me envision what new trails could look like and how to build them.

What does it take to build a trail, a digital product, or a marketing strategy? It takes planning, ground truthing, and problem-solving. What makes it easier and more fulfilling? When we use the right tools, collaborate, and share stories.

I am inspired by people and projects that bring communities together, encourage people to get outdoors, and have a positive impact on our world. If that sounds like you or something you're working on, reach out!
